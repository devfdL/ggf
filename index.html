About the event

Gema Gembira, an event that is plotted to be a reflection of the happy feeling for the people who need excitement. It is made with the noble intention to spread the happiness to the whole community with the joyfull music , Gema gembira is ready to reconcile the territory of the music event in Indonesia, especially at Depok.

Seeing the condition of the Depok city, which has been home to great music events for years, Mrl Creator is determined to restore the music euphoria in this Student City. With a lot of experience in organizing Music Events, we are determined to provide a place for the music community, the music lovers community as wellas the whole element in the occurrence of a music event in Depok.

About Gema Gembira Fest


in Gema Gembira, people will be presented with amazing music and stunning performers. This event is a celebration for musical and cultural diversity, inviting everyone to celebrate fun together in an atmosphere full of warmth and togetherness.
